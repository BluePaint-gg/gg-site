Guild Rankings

As you spend time with us and grow in levels, you will be given rankings within the guild. The more time and experience you contribute to the guild, the more priviliges will be given to you.

0 - 100,000 Experience

New guardian that has recently joined our ranks. A new guardian may recruit other guardians, store or take items from guild store, and see our guild advisor logs

 100,000 - 200,000 Experience

A guardian that has started to become familiar with our guild and its members. These guardians have the same priviliges as they did when they were newly recruited.

200,000 - 400,000 Experience

A guardian that has started to climb our ranks and grow in experience. Noble Paladins enjoy the same priviliges as they the ranks before it, but can also recall tagged items from other guild members.

400,000 - 800,000 Experience

A true hero of men. A guardian recruit that has dedicated fair amount of time to the guild and to the game. Their priviliges remain the same as a Noble Paladin

800,000 - 1,600,000 Experience

A guardian recruit who has excelled to even greater heights, and are very close to becoming a ranked guardian. They enjoy the same priviliges as Hero of Men

1,600,000 - 2,500,000 Experience

A guardian who has proven to be reliable and trustworthy. They hold the same priviliges as Leader of Men. Additionally, Loyal guardians may also initiate guild conflicts with other guilds.

2,500,000 - 4,000,000 Experience

An elite guardian who has trancended the rank of Loyal Guardian. Their rank priviliges remain the same as loyal guardians.

4,000,000 - 6,500,000 Experience

A guardian who has spent considerable time in the guild and have brought the guild to even greater heights.

6,500,000 - 10,000,000 Experience

A guardian who has forever etched their name in the guild. These guardians will forever remain within the ranks of GG. Guardian Immortals hold the same priviliges as Supreme Guardian, but they can also send mass messages to guild members.

10,000,000 - 15,000,000 Experience

A guardian who has trancended the rank of Guardian Immortal. They are very close to reaching the highest ranks amongst the guardians.

15,000,000+

A guardian who has risen to the pinnacle of ranks amongst the guild. These guardians may request for custom rankings.
